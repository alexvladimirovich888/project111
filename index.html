Internet Experiment

It started as a small idea between a few friends — a thought that maybe the internet doesn't need another app or platform, but something more absurd, more playful. So we made this. No roadmap, no investors, just an experiment to see how far a simple idea can go.

What does an experiment like this even need? Probably just a symbol everyone can recognize. We picked something almost too simple — a plain shape that anyone can use, twist, or meme. It's both the logo and the idea itself.
